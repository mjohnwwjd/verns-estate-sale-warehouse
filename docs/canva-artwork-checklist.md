# Canva Artwork Checklist

Use Canva as the artwork workbench, then export finished assets into `assets/img/`.

## Brand direction

- Dark charcoal or black sign background.
- Muted gray painted-wall texture.
- Warm yellow/gold block letters.
- Bold readable text for older customers.
- Decorative V mark with restrained scrollwork.
- Small red discount stamp accent is okay.
- Antique, warehouse, estate-sale, mid-century feel.
- Handmade sign character without looking messy.
- Primary wording: `Vern's Estate Sale Warehouse`. Use `Estate Sale`, not `Estate Sales`, for the warehouse brand.

## Do not accept

- Tiny text.
- Generic thrift app look.
- Corporate gradient hero art.
- Clip-art clutter.
- Logos that copy the uploaded reference photos exactly.
- Fine ornamental details that disappear on a phone.
- Low-contrast gray-on-black text.

## Promo video direction

- 8 to 15 seconds.
- Cartoon-style mid-century warehouse floor.
- Big readable title.
- Simple motion: rolling cart, lamp, dresser, yellow tags.
- End card: `Great Finds Every Week`.
- Export MP4 for the website and square/vertical cuts for social if needed.
